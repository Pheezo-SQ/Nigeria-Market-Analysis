🛍️ Nigeria E-Commerce Market Analysis

📌 Project Overview
A comprehensive market analysis of Nigeria's e-commerce landscape
using 1,000 transactions across 8 major platforms, 8 product
categories and 10 states. The project analyses platform
competition, consumer behaviour, regional patterns and
return rates.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- *Python* — Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
- *Jupyter Notebook* — Market EDA and analysis
- *Power BI Desktop* — Interactive market dashboard
- *Git & GitHub* — Version control

📊 Key Metrics
- Total Market Value: NGN 88.6 Million
- Total Transactions: 1,000
- Average Order Value: NGN 88,674
- Average Satisfaction: 3.68 / 5.0
- Overall Return Rate: 17.9%
- Mobile Shopping Rate: 72%

🔍 Key Findings
- Jumia leads with 30.9% market share
- Electronics is the highest revenue category
- Lagos dominates regional revenue at 35%+
- 72% of transactions happen on mobile devices
- 25-34 age group is the largest customer segment
- Same-day delivery drives highest satisfaction scores
- High discounts do not necessarily improve satisfaction

💡 Business Recommendations
1. Jumia dominates but satisfaction gap shows competitor opportunity
2. Invest in mobile-first UX — 72% of shoppers use mobile
3. Reduce Electronics return rate — biggest revenue but highest returns
4. Expand same-day delivery — strongest driver of satisfaction
5. Target 25-34 segment with personalised campaigns
6. Grow presence in Abuja and Rivers — strong growth potential
